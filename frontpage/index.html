Frequently Asked

Find answers about our platform, donation process, and project selection.

What is EcoFund?
EcoFund is a platform connecting donors with verified decarbonizing projects.
How do donations work?
Donations are distributed to chosen projects, with transparency on utilization.
Are contributions tax-deductible?
Donations to EcoFund may qualify for tax deductions depending on your location.
Can I volunteer?
We welcome volunteers to advocate and educate others about decarbonization.
Who verifies projects?
All projects are verified by third-party evaluators with expertise in sustainability.
How to contact support?
Our support team is available to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Need more info?

Our team is ready to help with any additional information you may need.
